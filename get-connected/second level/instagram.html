Instagram

@glutenfreegirl This is Shauna James Ahern again but this time you can actually see all of the amazing food she makes. On instagram she also breaks down exactly what you need to recreate these dishes.


@udisglutenfree This is the Udi’s brand that I always rave about. Following them is a great way to stay up to do date on their products as well as get ideas on how to use them. Udi’s is also really good about having contests to win products and are interactive with their followers


@glutenfreebylc This account is run by Laura Church who is gluten-free and loves to experiment with recipes and ideas. She likes to share healthy recipes featuring natural foods as well.


@glutenfreelady.nl This is Kati who has celiac and shares recipies on her instagram feed as well as her blog. She has some inventive ideas with new products.
