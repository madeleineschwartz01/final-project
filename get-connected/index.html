Get Connected

As part of the millennium generation, I am on my phone all the time. I am also on most social media sites and check all of them at least once a day. This includes Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Not obsessive, just curious.

Ever since I was diagnosed with Chron’s and went gluten-free about 4 years ago, I started educating myself. This went from Google searches to reading pamphlets and talking with my doctor. However, I also realized that there are great resources on social media that were available to me. I started following people on Twitter, Instagram, and Pintrest who were also gluten-free and had ideas to share. Here I’ve listed the people that I go to for new ideas or suggestions.
