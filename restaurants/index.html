My Favorites

Restaurants

After being in college for 3 years, I can truly verify that college food does suck. I have definitely eaten just cereal for dinner because there was nothing else remotely edible in sight. However this lack of good tasting food makes me go out and explore the city and what it has to offer. Now, I am a college-student with a practically non-existent budget, I don’t go out to eat a lot but thanks to friends and family, I do leave my dorm. I have found some amazing spots for lunch and dinner that are gluten-free that some people don’t even know about. Here, I have attached some descriptions, a photo, and the address of where I have had these great experiences. Try them out if you live or are visiting the city; you won’t regret it.
